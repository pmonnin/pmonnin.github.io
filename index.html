I am a PhD student in the ORPAILLEUR team of LORIA, under the supervision of Amedeo Napoli and Adrien Coulet. My work is part of the PractiKPharma project, funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR). It is focused on knowledge extraction and comparison for pharmacogenomics.
