Pierre Monnin

Current position

I am a researcher at Orange in Belfort (France), where I work on knowledge engineering for dataset management and knowledge graph construction and refinement.

Previous positions

I was a PhD student in the Orpailleur team of LORIA, under the supervision of Amedeo Napoli and Adrien Coulet.
I defended my thesis, entitled Matching and mining in knowledge graphs of the Web of data - Applications in pharmacogenomics, on December 16, 2020.
My work was part of the PractiKPharma project, funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR).

Interests

Knowledge Discovery - Matching - Knowledge Graphs - Semantic Web - Linked Open Data
